Ethical Use Only

SmartSploit is designed exclusively for legitimate security research, penetration testing, and educational purposes. Users must obtain proper authorization before testing any smart contracts or blockchain systems they do not own.

Unauthorized use of this framework against systems without explicit permission is illegal and unethical. Always follow responsible disclosure practices and comply with applicable laws.
